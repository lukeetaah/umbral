# Source verification log

**Last checked:** 2026-07-17
**Scope:** UMBRAL v0.1-alpha.2

## Inclusion rule

The evidence base uses peer-reviewed primary studies, clearly identified peer-reviewed reviews, official standards, and official technical documentation. Commercial summaries, wellness blogs, testimonials, frequency-product pages, videos, and AI-generated summaries are excluded.

## Searches performed

Focused searches were run in PubMed/NCBI for:

- auditory–gustatory crossmodal correspondence;
- multisensory flavor and taste–odor integration;
- music-evoked autobiographical memory;
- conditioned salivation;
- imagined and inferred taste representation;
- auditory omission and hierarchical prediction;
- auditory perceptual learning and transfer;
- visual-to-auditory sensory substitution;
- human second-order conditioning;
- binaural and monaural beats, ASSR, negative and mixed findings.

Seed PMIDs 20515002, 20952795, 27784634, 19240137, 29778624, and 36805499 were checked against PubMed metadata. Additional records and limits are documented row-by-row in `evidence-matrix.csv`. Exact sample sizes are omitted when they were not present in an accessible abstract or checked full-text method; they are never inferred.

## Official guidance checked

- WHO/ITU safe-listening standard and WHO safe-listening Q&A: browser software cannot calibrate ear-level SPL; UMBRAL therefore combines conservative internal gain, a device-volume warning, brief stimuli, breaks, and an immediate Stop.
- Supabase Free documentation: two active free projects, 500 MB database, 5 GB egress, and inactivity pausing remain relevant; restoration policy can change and must be reviewed before launch.
- Vercel Hobby documentation and terms: the plan is USD 0 but restricted to personal, non-commercial use and pauses service at included limits. An institutional or commercial deployment must use another zero-cost/self-hosted route rather than silently violating terms or enabling billing.
- WCAG 2.2/WAI techniques: focus visibility, keyboard operation, target size, reflow, and reduced motion guide the interface audit.

## Known gaps

This is a targeted MVP search, not a systematic review. It does not estimate publication bias, run duplicate independent screening, or assess risk of bias with a formal instrument. Before participant recruitment, a protocol-specific systematic search, ethics review, and preregistration are required.
