# Protocol v0.1 — Sound–color acquisition

## Primary question

Does a newly generated auditory cue produce above-sham, repeatable selection of a paired color after the color is removed?

## Sequence

Baseline → paired A1+C1 → hidden repeat → sham → fading → omission → transfer A2→A1 → A2 test → delayed retest.

## Measures

Occurrence-first response, forced choice only afterward, confidence 0–100, expectation 0–100, latency, adverse event, and free text under separate consent.

## Failure criteria

No advantage over sham; instability at retest; dependence on explicit expectation; excessive attrition; or any safety signal that makes continuation unjustified.
