# Statistical analysis plan

1. Validate hashes, protocol versions, missingness, duplicate sessions, and sham balance.
2. Report completion, exclusions, adverse events, and distributions before outcomes.
3. Estimate within-person paired-minus-sham effects with bootstrap 95% intervals.
4. Estimate retest stability without treating ordinal confidence as interval precision unless sensitivity agrees.
5. Fit transparent regression with expectation and trial order; publish coefficients and residual checks.
6. Label subgroup, anomaly, and adaptive analyses exploratory.
7. Report effect sizes and uncertainty regardless of p-values; do not claim neural activation.
