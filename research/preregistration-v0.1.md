# Preregistration v0.1

- Population: consenting adults in nonclinical online use; demo data excluded from collective analysis unless explicitly contributed.
- Primary outcome: within-person paired-minus-sham target similarity at immediate test.
- Secondary: seven-day reproducibility, spontaneous occurrence, confidence, expectation dependence.
- Exclusions: failed audio check, incomplete primary block, protocol error; all exclusions reported.
- Stopping: predetermined sample/capacity limit or safety review. No optional stopping on significance.
- Confirmatory contrasts: paired vs sham; retest vs chance; paired effect adjusted for expectation.
- Negative and null results are published in `negative-results.md`.
